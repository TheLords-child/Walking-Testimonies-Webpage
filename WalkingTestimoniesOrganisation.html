Aims and Objectives

Curious about what we as an organisation strive to complete? Look below and learn about our mission and vision.

Our Mission

Our mission is to mobilise the youth of South Africa to actively engage in service projects that will provide relief and support to the poor and homeless.

We commit to creating outreach engagements that will assist us in preaching the Gospel, to give and to win souls back to the Kingdom of God, all rooted in the principles of compassion and community.

We strive to uplift those in need by embodying the teachings of Jesus Christ, fostering a spirit of generosity, and building lasting relationships within our communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower South African youth to become catalysts for change, fostering a society where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, is not restricted access to the Gospel or to basic needs and the support necessary to live a faith life.

We envision communities where hope is restored, lives are transformed, and the love of Christ is reflected in our actions.

Our Programs

We are excited to offer a variety of programs that aim to empower the youth, spread the Gospel, and make a positive impact in the lives of those in need. Below are some of our key programs:

  • Bible Studies: Engaging in regular Bible study sessions to deepen faith and understanding of the Gospel.
  • Community Outreach: Participating in projects that help to uplift and support the homeless, poor, and underserved communities.
  • Camps: Providing spiritual retreats and camps for youth to grow in their faith and develop leadership skills.
  • Scripture Distribution: Spreading the Word of God through the distribution of Bibles and other Christian literature.
  • Worship Events: Hosting worship events and services to foster spiritual growth and community bonding.
  • Marches for Christ: Organizing marches to raise awareness and proclaim the name of Jesus Christ in public spaces.

Benefits of Our Programs

Our programs offer immense value and benefits, including:

  • Encouraging spiritual growth and a deeper understanding of the Bible.
  • Fostering a sense of community, unity, and compassion among the youth.
  • Building leadership skills and empowering young people to make a positive difference.
  • Providing opportunities for personal growth, service, and outreach to those in need.

These programs have a transformative impact on the lives of young people, helping them live out their faith in practical ways, and making a lasting difference in the world.
